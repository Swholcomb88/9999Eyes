A seeded anthology. Every number becomes a word, every word becomes a life — told through the eyes of a soul who's lived them all.
Live site: add your GitHub Pages link here once deployed
How it works
A number is chosen.
That number seeds a random word generator against an English wordlist.
The word becomes the muse for a short story — any era, any place, real or invented — written in first person, through that life's eyes.
Each life gets a chapter title, a tombstone-style header image, and its own page in the archive.
Some lives get eyes carved into their stone. Some don't.
